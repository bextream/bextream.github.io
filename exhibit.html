I spent a lot of time in the garage as a kid. It was full of stuff collected by my dad, justified by 'that'll be useful someday'. And a lot of it has been. Combined with things I learned from him, and my own experiments, I would tinker among the stuff. Find things and envision something for it. Or look for something to fit a purpose, but not sure exactly what will turn up.

My dad also taught me about what things were and did, many pretexted in frustration with 'did you know that was a...' as he held it up in its new transformed state.

Just over 2 years ago in the old garage cleanout, a few oddments inspired me to build some lamp. Prompted by a desire to create, I began this current body of work. It's been a lot of work but all enjoyable and I hope you will enjoy them equally. I dedicate this exhibition to my Dad.
