Using a steel kettle was an idea I thought of, I just needed to wait until one turned up that was right, and it did. I had already picked up the stand from a junk shop. It was missing a leg but otherwise ok. Manufacturing the timber and metal brackets for the third leg was a task in itself. Using the bike forks to hold the pot was inspired while rummaging junk looking for something to do the job. The final details were the illuminated coffee level, the light mount and the repurpose of the strength control knob for a switch.

Free standing lamp, 12V 10W Halogen.
