This lamp was the product of playing around with a range of bits I had collected. I liked the two chrome spheres and thought they would fit being able to move about on the flexible goosenecks. The uneven necks were a product of what I had but worked well. I just needed to have something to mount them on.
The old laser control case was perfect, fitting with the lamps and also stable. I mounted an old 3 way switch to allow independent control of the lamps.

Table Lamp, lamps independently operated. 12V 2x3WG4 LED replacement globes
