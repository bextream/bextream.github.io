The mannequin torso was originally painted gold so the paint needed to
be stripped off. There were several concepts around the stand for this lamp. I already had the drum stand and was only going to use the bottom half. The initial idea was to light this from the bottom of the leg with white light. This didn't adequately illuminate the whole torso and looked strange.
So I opted for either the heart region
or the lower back. The decision for
the rgb selectors came later. There became a Giger-esk feel to it, adding the shower hose and using an old colander for the control panel around the leg.

Free standing Floor Lamp, 7.5V 300mA, rgb colour selectors to mix
an array of colour luminance.
