This is my first attempt to create a lamp considering what a specific person may like. I had considered using the hands in this fashion since finding them some time ago. The base took much iteration, much consideration and time. Drilling the glass dome for the sockets and switch proved relatively straightforward. Complexity was the socket and light mounts and fitting all electrics discreetly in a small space.

Table lamp, 12V 4x300mA led globes.
