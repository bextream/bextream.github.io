I admit I have a penchant for orange retro colouring... the wok caught my attention but internal need vs want briefly cast it off. then I thought that it could be an interesting lamp. It was akin to film lighting. I found the stand a little broken and rickety. With some repair and addition of a plumming joint. Something I kept as it looked interesting and for its practical implications. A brake/tail car globe socket, this allowed for two level luminance. The last details to evolve was converting the original plug into the switch and power input. Using the handle from the lid as the switch.

Free standing lamp, 12V 500mA,
two stage brightness, led replacement brake/tail globe.
