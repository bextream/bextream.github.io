This concept I had already implemented into a freestanding lamp. What I needed to do was find the right body and base. I considered many options though chose the current lamp body from a part I had laying about. The switch turned by the tap has varied luminance controlled by a dimmer with a tap spindle modified to turn the switch. The base had several iterations of brick chunks. Then with an idea in mind, a long wait to find some tiles that worked. Once I found them I poured the concrete base and set the tiles. This was just right to weight the lamp and finish off the piece.

Desk Lamp, 12V 20W Halogen globe.
