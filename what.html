Why lamps... longstanding love of interesting lamps, over the years building a few and restoring many others. I build a lot of things, always have, and generally from recycled materials. I enjoy the process, the conceptualisation and construction. Creating something interesting yet practical out of discarded things.

Just over 2 years ago in an old garage cleanout, a few oddments inspired me to build another lamp. Contribution of various factors has seen
it quickly turn to obsession. Each lamp is built considering every detail, and an expectation it will keep that form for some time longer. Each powered by between 12V and 5V, overall max
of 20 Watt but many much less. Underlying this is the proviso it's built with mainly recycled and reused materials.The names? people who reacted strongest, or inspired or influenced
any specific lamp.
